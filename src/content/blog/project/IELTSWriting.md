# 雅思大作文 Band 7+ 万能论据库 (Customized Version)

---

### 1. 社会类万能论据 (Social / Government / Environment)
**适用范围**：公共政策、环保、交通、教育改革、城市规划、医疗福利等。
**核心设定**：南京（Nanjing）作为试点城市。

> **Template:**
> "Take **Nanjing**, a prominent provincial capital in Eastern China, as a case in point. The city initiated a pilot scheme focusing on **[Insert Policy/Measure]** in 2021 to address emerging social challenges. This strategic move yielded tangible benefits. According to data released by the **Nanjing Municipal Bureau of Statistics** in 2023, the city witnessed a **[Insert Number]%** improvement in **[Insert Metric of Improvement]** after the implementation of this policy."

**填空示例 (Example for Traffic):**
*   **[Insert Policy/Measure]**: imposing congestion charges (征收拥堵费)
*   **[Insert Number]**: 35%
*   **[Insert Metric of Improvement]**: traffic efficiency during rush hours (高峰期交通效率)

---

### 2. 企业类万能论据 (Business / Work / Technology)
**适用范围**：远程办公、科技对工作影响、企业管理、团队协作、创新竞争等。
**核心设定**：大疆（DJI）作为行业巨头。

> **Template:**
> "A compelling example can be found in **DJI**, a global leader in civilian drones and aerial imaging technology. Two years ago, the corporation integrated **[Insert Working Method/Technology]** into its daily operations to optimize workflow. The outcome was remarkably positive. Their subsequent annual fiscal report indicated that the **[Insert KPI: Productivity/Profit/Efficiency]** had surged by **[Insert Number]%**, directly attributed to the adoption of this approach."

**填空示例 (Example for Remote Work):**
*   **[Insert Working Method/Technology]**: flexible remote working policies (灵活远程办公政策)
*   **[Insert KPI]**: overall staff productivity (员工整体生产力)
*   **[Insert Number]**: 28%

---

### 3. 个人生活类万能论据 (Personal / Lifestyle / Psychology)
**适用范围**：家庭关系、幸福感、Gap Year、消费习惯、健康生活、儿童教育等。
**核心设定**：权威期刊《社会心理学杂志》的纵向调查。

> **Template:**
> "This argument is further supported by a longitudinal study published in **'The Journal of Social Psychology'**. The researchers conducted a comprehensive survey tracking 2,000 individuals to investigate the impact of **[Insert Personal Choice/Behavior]**. The findings revealed a strong correlation: over **[Insert Number]%** of the respondents who **[Insert Action Taken]** reported significantly higher levels of **[Insert Psychological Benefit]** compared to their counterparts."

**填空示例 (Example for Gap Year):**
*   **[Insert Personal Choice/Behavior]**: taking a gap year before university (上大学前间隔年)
*   **[Insert Number]**: 82%
*   **[Insert Action Taken]**: utilized this time for travel and volunteering (利用这段时间旅行和志愿者服务)
*   **[Insert Psychological Benefit]**: academic resilience and self-awareness (学术抗压能力和自我认知)
